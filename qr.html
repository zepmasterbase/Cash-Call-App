Demo notes

• This demo generates an ephemeral token client-side. In production: generate signed, short-lived tokens on your server and embed them in the QR. The backend must verify the token before allowing any wallet deduction.
• The payer's app will decode the QR and call your backend endpoint (e.g. POST /api/payments/peer-deduct) with the token. Your server validates and debits the payee's wallet.
Local demo — payloads stored in browser storage only.
